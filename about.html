About Estimator

It was designed by me (Sylvester) owner of Sylvest Ventures and Detwins Innovations .


Estimator helps people in business to calculate percentage amount to give a middlemen (someone that brings contracts or business to you) who brings business deals to them and also calculates the total money on the deal after the middlemen has been paid.
